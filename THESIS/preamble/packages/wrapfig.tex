%%% remarks
%-- \begin{wrapfigure}[number of narrow lines]{placement}[overhang]{width} ... \end{wrapfigure}
%-- overhang defines the width of the figure using space of the margins
%-- for position there are the following options (large letters result in floating figures, small letters place the figure exactly here):
%-- r R – the right side of the text
%-- l L – the left side of the text
%-- i I – the inside edge–near the binding (if [twoside] document)
%-- o O – the outside edge–far from the binding
%-- all these things are also possible with wraptable
